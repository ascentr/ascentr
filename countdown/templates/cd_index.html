COUNTDOWN

The Count Down game is the online version of the famous TV quiz game broadcast on Channel 4 in the UK. You can choose to play the Word Game, Numbers Game or the solve the anagram Conundrum. You are allowed 35 seconds the extra 5 seconds are needed for plying the on the screen.

Word Game

Choose 9 letters as vowels or consonants and make the longest word you can. You can not use any letter more than once. Points are scored based on the length of the word that is found in the dictionary.

Numbers Game

You choose 6 large & small numbers and the game generates a target answer. You can then use the in-game calculator to try and get the answer. 10 Points are given for the correct answer and the time left is added to give bonus points. You are only allowed to choose a maximum of 4 large numbers from (25, 50, 75, 100).

The Conundrum

The conundrum is a 9 letter anagram that has been shuffled by the game. You get 10 points for solving the conundrum and time left is added as a bonus.
